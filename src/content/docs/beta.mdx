Backplane Beta Program is currently by **invite only**.

If you wish to participate in this program you can register your interest on our [Slack Channel](https://backplane-dev.slack.com/archives/C07CSJYU2QH)

## Benefits

| Benefit               | Description                                                                |
| :-------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Exclusive Access      | Be among the first to experience and shape the future of cloud integration |
| Direct Support        | Get dedicated support from our team to maximise your Backplane experience  |
| Influence Development | our feedback will directly impact our features and roadmap.                |
