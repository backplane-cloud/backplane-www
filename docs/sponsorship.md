Backplane Software provides a separate hosted SaaS offering and focuses on developing extension modules available to organisations under license.

### Benefits explained

#### Direct Access to Backplane Dev Team

> You will be provided with exclusive access to a private Slack Channel where you can chat to the Backplane Open Source team.

#### Exclusive Early Access

> As the team line-up releases, you'll have the opportunity to try these out before GA.

#### Feature Roadmap planning invites

> The Backplane Open Source Development team will meet to discuss future Features for development. These meetings will pass the mic to our sponsors for guidance, feedback to how we can make Backplane better.

#### Feature Voting rights

> Features that are approved by the Product Owner for development will be entered onto the Product Backlog, and Feature delivery will be prioritised according to what adds the most value and benefit. Sponsors have a unique opportunity to vote for Features they would like to see delivered sooner.
